### `Latest`
# Version 2.0
## Multi-language support, Japanese card images & Wishlist
**Version 2.0 – March 2026**

---

### 🌍 Visual Sealed Product Catalog (FR / DE / ES / IT / PT)
French, German, Spanish, Italian and Portuguese users can now browse sealed products in their language when adding items — no need to type English names. Select your flag, pick from a pre-loaded visual catalog with box art and prices, tap to add. Done.

### 🇯🇵 Japanese Set Card Images
The Market set browser now shows full card artwork for Japanese-exclusive sets — including Pokémon Card 151 (sv2a), White Flare, Black Bolt, and all recent SV-era JP expansions. Powered by TCGDex.

### 💛 Wishlist
Save cards from the Market browser to a personal wishlist. Tap ♡ on any card in a set to add it — the heart fills orange to confirm. Revisit your wishlist anytime from the Market tab.

### ⚡ Quick Add from Market
Tap + on any card in the set browser to open the Add Item sheet with name, set, number, rarity and card image already filled in. One tap to add, no manual typing.

### 💶 Multi-Region Price Breakdown
Card details now show EN, FR, DE, ES and IT Cardmarket prices side by side — so you always know what your card is worth in each market at a glance.

### 🔗 View on Cardmarket
Every card and sealed product now has a direct link to its Cardmarket listing. One tap opens the product page in your browser.

### 🔄 Bulk Price Refresh *(Premium)*
Refresh Cardmarket prices for your entire collection in one tap from the Items tab header. All cards update in parallel — perfect for a weekly portfolio check.

# Version 1.2
## Cardmarket price integration & Market browser
**Version 1.2 – March 2026**

---

### 💰 Live Cardmarket Prices
- Tap **Get Market Price** on any card to fetch the current Cardmarket lowest price instantly
- Grade-aware pricing: PSA 10, PSA 9, CGC 10 prices fetched separately from raw NM
- French, German, Spanish and Italian market prices tracked individually per card
- **Auto price refresh** (Premium): prices update automatically in the background every 24 hours

### 🛍️ Market Tab & Set Browser
- New Market tab: browse the latest Pokémon TCG set releases
- Tap any set to view the full card list with images, rarity and Cardmarket prices
- Powered by Cardmarket data via TCGGo

### 📋 Card Detail Improvements
- Price history chart directly inside each card's detail view
- Track Market button: log a Cardmarket price entry in one tap
- Mark as Sold: quickly record a sale from the card detail screen
- Card image shown in the hero when a photo has been attached

### 🏷️ Collector Number & Rarity
- Card number (e.g. 177/132) stored and displayed on every card
- Rarity badge (Common, Rare, Double Rare, etc.) shown in the collection grid
