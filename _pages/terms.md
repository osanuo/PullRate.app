## Description of Service
PullRate is a Pokemon TCG portfolio tracking application for iOS devices that helps users:

- Add and manage a personal card collection with grades, prices, and quantities
- Track the market value of each card over time with price history entries
- Record sales transactions and calculate profit and ROI
- View portfolio analytics including total value, monthly gains, and category breakdowns
- Export collection and sales data as a CSV file
- Optionally sync data across devices via iCloud
